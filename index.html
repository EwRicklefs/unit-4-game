You will be given a random number at the start of the game (target).
There are four posible crystals to choose from, each one adds a specific amount of points to you total score.
You win by getting your point total to match the target score.
The crystal values are hidden, you must figure them out from what they add to your total.
Each new game will change the values of the crystals
